OVERVIEW

Farm x Table is an autonomous delivery service that brings the farm to the home done for Design Innovation 181: Reimagining Mobility at UC Berkeley lead by Professor Purin Phanichphant in Spring 2019. I worked with Victoria Wu and Andrew Louie. The project investigates the usage of autonomous vehicles in applications other than transporting humans.

My role was leading the ideation phase and prototyping, which involved designing prototype vehicle and wireframing. Victoria produced the mobile app prototype and Andrew Louie focused on economic analysis.


PROBLEM


We began with a top down approach using a mind-map to direct our area of interest and decided a focus on food related autonomous vehicles. We selected interviewees Ashley, who worked in a gelato store; Bryan, a software engineer that could provide perspective on challenges and context of the autonomous vehicle field; and Yuki, a general college student who represents a potential population of interest.

IDEATION

how might we:

Use autonomous food services to address peak hour loads

how might we:

Use autonomous vehicle delivery to provide fresher and uncommon produce

how might we:

Use AV to deliver quality food while maintaining convenience



We used our how might we statements to guide our brainstorming groupings and used a series of short 5-minute session and mini-discussions to create ideas relevant to each statement.




Each group member chose a how might we and idea to create a one-page expansions of initial ideas. After discussion, we decided to move forward with "A Moveable Feast." A one-pager based on the idea of bringing the farm to the user to provide fresher produce.

EARLY
PROTOTYPE

Our early experiential prototype usesda paper app to present screens necessary to call the truck. We then made a low-fi verson of the truck using a whiteboard. We emphasized the experience of the user picking the vegetables themselves.


HIGH FIDELITY
PROTOTYPE

To prototype and test our farm x table truck concept in higher fidelity, we paired an application prototype that calls the farm to the user with renderings that describe the process of appproaching, entering, and leaivng the farm x table truck.
